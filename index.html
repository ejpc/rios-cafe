Welcome to Rio's

Right on Manningtree High Street, Rio's is the cosy spot for breakfast, lunch and a proper cuppa. Pull up a chair inside, soak up the sun outside, or grab and go from our takeaway window. Everything is made fresh to order — and well-behaved dogs are always welcome.

Questions & Answers

Do I need to book a table?

No need — just pop in. For larger groups, give us a ring on 01206 393100 and we'll save you a spot.

Are dogs allowed?

Yes! Well-behaved dogs are always welcome, inside and out.

Do you do takeaway?

We do — our takeaway window is open daily from 8:30am for drinks and food on the go.

Do you serve breakfast all day?

Yes, breakfast is served all day, every day.

Do you cater for veggie, vegan or gluten free?

We do — lots of dishes can be made veggie or vegan, and gluten free bread is available. We're a small kitchen so please tell us about any allergies.

How do I order?

Sitting inside? We offer table service. Sitting outside? Just order at the bar with your table number.
